# Job Specification: Marketing and Sales Manager

**Company:** Caricom Connects: Driving Integration and Empowerment  
**Location:** Trinidad and Tobago  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time

## Position Summary

Caricom Connects is seeking a Marketing and Sales Manager to lead marketing efforts and drive sales growth. The Marketing and Sales Manager will play a crucial role in promoting the platform, acquiring customers, and expanding the company's market presence within the Caricom region.

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Marketing Strategy:**
   - Develop and implement marketing strategies to promote the Caricom Connects platform.
   - Identify target markets and segments, and tailor marketing campaigns accordingly.
2. **Lead Generation and Customer Acquisition:**

   - Generate leads and acquire new customers through various channels, including digital marketing, events, and partnerships.
   - Nurture leads through the sales funnel and convert them into paying customers.

3. **Brand Management:**

   - Manage the company's brand identity and ensure consistency across all marketing channels and communications.
   - Monitor market trends and competitor activities to identify opportunities for differentiation.

4. **Sales Growth and Revenue Generation:**
   - Set sales targets and develop sales plans to achieve revenue goals.
   - Build and maintain relationships with key clients and stakeholders to drive sales growth.

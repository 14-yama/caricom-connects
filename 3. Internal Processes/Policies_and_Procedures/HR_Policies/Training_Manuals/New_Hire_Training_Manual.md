# New Hire Training Manual

Welcome to Caricom Connects! This training manual is designed to provide you with the essential information and resources you need to succeed in your role. Whether you're joining us as a Sales Associate, Procurement Agent, Customer Service Representative, or in any other capacity, this manual will help you get acquainted with our company, culture, policies, and procedures.

## Table of Contents:

## 1. Introduction to Caricom Connects:

### Mission and Vision:

At Caricom Connects, our mission is to facilitate trade, foster community, and promote tourism within the Caricom region. We strive to build a vibrant and inclusive community where businesses and individuals can thrive together. Our vision is to create an interconnected ecosystem that empowers growth, collaboration, and innovation.

### Core Values:

Our core values guide everything we do at Caricom Connects:

- Collaboration
- Integrity
- Innovation
- Inclusivity
- Sustainability

## 2. Company Policies and Procedures:

### Equal Employment Opportunity:

Caricom Connects is an equal opportunity employer and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, national origin, age, genetic information, disability, or veteran status.

### Code of Conduct:

We expect all employees to adhere to our code of conduct, which includes principles of professionalism, integrity, respect, and confidentiality. Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that reflects positively on the company and its values.

### Health and Safety:

We prioritize the health and safety of our employees and customers. All employees are required to comply with health and safety regulations and follow established safety protocols to ensure a safe work environment.

### Confidentiality:

Employees must maintain the confidentiality of sensitive information, including proprietary business information, client data, and personal employee information. Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information is prohibited.

## 3. Role-Specific Training:

### Sales Associate Training:

- Learn about our products and services, including features, benefits, and pricing.
- Develop sales skills and techniques to effectively engage with customers and drive sales.
- Familiarize yourself with sales processes, including order processing and customer follow-up.

### Procurement Agent Training:

- Understand our procurement processes, including sourcing, negotiation, and order management.
- Learn to operate lorry or heavy T vehicles and follow designated routes for transportation.
- Develop skills in handling heavy products, performing quality checks, and managing inventory.

### Customer Service Representative Training:

- Familiarize yourself with the Glorious Helpdesk platform and our customer service procedures.
- Develop communication skills to provide exceptional customer support via phone, email, and other channels.
- Learn how to troubleshoot customer issues, document interactions, and escalate unresolved issues as needed.

## 4. Tools and Resources:

### Employee Handbook:

Refer to the Employee Handbook for detailed information on company policies, procedures, benefits, and expectations.

### Training Materials:

Access training materials and resources provided by the company to support your learning and development.

### Glorious Helpdesk:

Utilize the Glorious Helpdesk platform for managing and resolving customer inquiries and issues efficiently.

## 5. Onboarding Checklist:

- Complete required paperwork, including tax forms, employment contracts, and company policies acknowledgment.
- Attend orientation sessions to learn about company culture, values, and expectations.
- Participate in role-specific training sessions to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge for your position.
- Familiarize yourself with company tools, resources, and systems, including Glorious Helpdesk.
- Meet with your manager or mentor to discuss goals, expectations, and performance objectives.

Congratulations on joining the Caricom Connects team! We're excited to have you on board and look forward to your contributions to our success.

If you have any questions or need assistance during your onboarding process, please don't hesitate to reach out to your manager or the Human Resources department.
